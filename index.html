The Problem

IEQ is Invisible, But Costly

Many assume air, temperature, light, and sound have little to do with how we perform at work. But these factors, when overlooked, quietly erode wellbeing and output, a phenomenon known as Sick Building Syndrome (SBS), symptoms like headaches, fatigue, and brain fog that lead to absenteeism and cognitive loss.

The Hidden Cost

How Much Is Poor IEQ Really Costing You?

From productivity loss to avoidable sick days, IEQ has real economic impact.

Real-World Impact

Improved indoor air quality could save the U.S. economy billions annually through reduced illness and enhanced cognitive function.

Why It Matters

Higher cognitive performance means better focus, faster decision‐making, and fewer errors. Even a small percent gain can translate to huge productivity wins when scaled across a workforce.
